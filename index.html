Log

    Actions

    Night Slip

    Turn-based isometric slasher escape.

    • Click reachable blue tiles to move.
    • Yellow tiles are interactables (Exit, Vault, Alarm).
    • Red tiles show killer threat.
    • Use E Interact, H Hide, Shift Sprint, Space End Turn.
    • Press T to toggle autoplay tester.

    How to Play

    You have 2 AP per turn. Move costs 1 AP, Sprint costs 2 AP (+noise), Hide costs 1 AP, Interact costs 1 AP.

    After first hit, you are injured and move range is reduced by 1 tile.

    The killer patrols, investigates sound, chases on sight, and searches when vision is lost.
